How This Works

This scroll-triggered image sequence creates a cinematic experience as you navigate through the page. Each frame is synchronized with your scroll position, creating smooth, frame-by-frame animation.

This demo uses procedurally generated frames, but you can replace them with your own image sequence. Simply upload your frames to a hosting service and update the image URLs in the code.

Perfect for product reveals, storytelling, and creating engaging landing pages that captivate your audience.
